About Us

How Did we Get Our Name

At CutCofy, we believe every startup deserves a shot at greatness. The big players in the industry didn’t get there by luck—they followed smart strategies, built strong brands, and learned from every step along the way. That’s where we come in.

We dive into the success stories of industry leaders, study their paths, and figure out what made them thrive. Then, we help startups apply those same winning strategies—tailored to fit their own journey. It's like having a blueprint for success but with your unique spin.

The name CutCofy comes from this very idea: we cut through the complexities, learn from the best, and help startups copy the most effective parts to create their own success stories, of course with many cups of coffee. From building standout websites to designing logos that tell your story, we’re here to guide you every step of the way.

We’re not just here to consult—we’re here to help you succeed, faster and smarter, with strategies that work. At CutCofy, your success is our mission.
